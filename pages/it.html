Becoming a programmer - Jacques

While studying horsemanship in the US, I become involved with producing web-based educational materials for a company leading the digital horsemanship community. The training company was very progressive using IT processes throughout most of their business. I was hooked and hungry to learn more. I am now back in school at Santa Fe College to study.

Important design feature while building this web site.

  • Responsive design - The site will adjust to the size of the screen.
  • Horizontal and mobile menu - On the desktop view, “Projects” is a link. However, when in the mobile view, a “Projects list” is necessary to get to the projects page.
  • Image rollover on the bicycle page - Also resizes to adjust to various screen sizes
  • Light box image swap - On the Dior page, a 5 images swap was used. The thumbnails were resized to insure they were on one line regardless of screen size.
  • Links - The size of some of the links was increased and displayed in blue on the mobile screen size to make it easier to use.
  • Tables - The table was challenging to fit on a phone size screen. The first column was deleted on the portrait view, but still there on landscape view.
